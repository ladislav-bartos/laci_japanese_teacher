# Day 1 (1日目): 意味がたくさんある言葉① (Words which have many meanings 1)[cite: 12]

## Vocabulary — Words with Multiple Meanings: 切る / 切れる (to cut / to break)[cite: 12]
* **ひもが切れる (きれる)** — the string breaks[cite: 12] ↔ **ひもを切る (きる)** — cut the string[cite: 12]
* **電池が切れる (でんち)** — the battery dies[cite: 12]
* **タバコが切れる** — be out of cigarettes[cite: 12]
* **賞味期限が切れる (しょうみきげん)** — past the best before date[cite: 12]
* **しびれが切れる** — legs gone to sleep / legs are numb[cite: 12]
* **電源を切る (でんげん)** — switch off[cite: 12] ↔ **電源が切れる** — power goes off[cite: 12]
* **野菜の水気を切る (やさいのみずけ)** — dry the vegetables (remove water)[cite: 12]
* **スタートを切る** — start off[cite: 12]
* **100メートル競走で10秒を切る (きょうそうで10びょう)** — run 100 meters in less than 10 seconds[cite: 12]
* **ハンドルを右に切る (みぎ)** — turn right (the steering wheel)[cite: 12]
* **カードをよく切る** — shuffle the cards[cite: 12]
* **キレる <俗語>** — lose one's temper / snap (e.g., キレて犯罪を犯す)[cite: 12]

## Vocabulary — Words with Multiple Meanings: つく / つける (to attach / to apply)[cite: 12]
* **しみがつく** — get a stain on[cite: 12] ↔ **しみをつける** — put a stain on[cite: 12]
* **窓ガラスに水滴がつく (まどガラスにすいてき)** — there is condensation on the window[cite: 12]
* **利子がつく (りし)** — yield interest[cite: 12]
* **身につく (み)** — master (a skill)[cite: 12] ↔ **身につける** — acquire (a skill)[cite: 12]
* **力がつく (ちから)** — gain skills / power[cite: 12] ↔ **力をつける** — build strength[cite: 12]
* **差がつく (さ)** — pull ahead / a gap opens up[cite: 12] ↔ **差をつける** — make a difference[cite: 12]
* **見当がつく (けんとう)** — guess / have an idea[cite: 12] ↔ **見当をつける** — make a guess[cite: 12]
* **めどがつく** — there is hope for / an end is in sight[cite: 12] ↔ **めどをつける** — set a goal / prospect[cite: 12]
* **決心がつく (けっしん)** — make a decision[cite: 12] ↔ **決心する** — to decide[cite: 12]
* **服ににおいがつく (ふく)** — clothes pick up an odor[cite: 12]
* **折り目がつく (おりめ)** — get a crease[cite: 12] ↔ **折り目をつける** — put a crease in[cite: 12]
* **味がつく (あじ)** — become flavored[cite: 12] ↔ **味をつける** — flavor[cite: 12]

## Vocabulary — Words with Multiple Meanings: 当たる / 当てる (to hit / to guess)[cite: 12]
* **ボールが当たる (あたる)** — the ball hits...[cite: 12] ↔ **ボールを当てる (あてる)** — hit with a ball[cite: 12]
* **答えが当たる (こたえ)** — get the right answer[cite: 12] ↔ **答えを当てる** — guess the answer[cite: 12]
* **宝くじが当たる (たからくじ)** — win a lottery[cite: 12]
* **日が当たる (ひ)** — get sun[cite: 12] ↔ **日を当てる** — expose to the sun[cite: 12]
* **額に手を当てる (ひたいにて)** — put your hand on your forehead[cite: 12]
