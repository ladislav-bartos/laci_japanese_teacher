**Notes on the misses:**
- **往復 (#4)**, not 回復 — 往復 = round trip (there and back); 回復 = recovery/recuperation. "実家

| # | Question | Your answer | Correct | ✅/❌ |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 引っ越す／引っ越した＋かいがあって | b | b | ✅ |
| 2 | 減らして／減らした＋まで | b | a | ❌ |
| 3 | 教え／教えた＋がいがある | b | a | ❌ |
| 4 | かいがあって／かいもなく（手術、戻せず） | a | b | ❌ |
| 5 | 名前まで／までに＋変えて | b | a | ❌ |

**Notes on the misses:**
- **#2** — correct is **a** (減らして), not 減らした. 〜てまで needs the て-form directly (Vて＋まで),
  not the plain past (Vた). 給料を減らしてまで, not 給料を減らしたまで.
- **#3** — correct is **a** (教え), not 教えた. 〜がい attaches to the ます-stem (教え＋がい＝
  教えがい), not the plain past form.
- **#4** — correct is **b** (かいもなく), not かいがあって. The result stated is negative ("could
  not get back to his original form/condition") — かいもなく ("in vain") matches an unsuccessful
  outcome; かいがあって would imply the surgery *worked*, contradicting "取り戻せなかった."
- **#5** — correct is **a** (まで), not までに. 〜てまで takes まで directly after the て-form
  (名前まで変えて); までに is a different pattern (a deadline marker, "by the time X").

**This section is worth prioritizing:** every miss (#2,3,4,5) is a connection-form or meaning
mix-up specific to today's four new patterns (かいがある／かいもなく／がい／てまで) — normal for a
first exposure, but worth a deliberate side-by-side review before Day 2.
