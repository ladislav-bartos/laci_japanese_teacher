**Notes on the misses:**
- **#3/#4 (っぽい↔気味 swapped both ways)** — 水っぽい ("watery-tasting") is a fixed collocation
  describing a *quality* an object has, which is っぽい's job; 寝不足気味 ("a touch of sleep
  deprivation") describes a *state you're in*, which is 気味's job. You picked the opposite pattern
  in both directions — worth drilling these two side by side specifically.
- **#7** — correct is **a** (もん), not ものの. 「だって、おいしいんだもん」gives an emphatic reason
  (もの/もん＝から); ものの is concessive ("although"), which doesn't answer "why are you eating
  that again?"
- **#8** — correct is **a** (ものの), not ものなら. ものなら needs a potential-form verb (行ける
  ものなら, 戻れるものなら); it can't attach directly to a plain adjective like 軽い the way you
  used it here.
- **#9** — correct is **a** (はもとより), not はともかく. 「ランチはもとより、ディナーも」praises
  both things ("of course lunch, but dinner too"); はともかく means "setting X aside," which
  contradicts a sentence that's praising both.
- **#11** — correct is **a** (まだしも), not 抜きにしても. まだしも is the "X would be tolerable,
  but Y is too much" comparison-of-degree pattern, which fits "a short wait, but not an hour";
  抜きにして means "leaving X out entirely," a different idea.
- **#15** — correct is **a** (てかなわない), not てならない. てかなわない is for external annoyances
  you're troubled by (noise, inconvenience); てならない is reserved for feelings that arise from
  *inside* you (心配でならない, 気になってならない). A neighbor's TV is an external nuisance, not
  an inner feeling.
- **#18** — correct is **a** (こともない), not ことはない. 「1日5つならこもられないこともない」
  expresses tentative possibility ("might not be impossible"); ことはない denies something more
  flatly and reads awkwardly stacked with a "if it's just X" conditional like this one.
- **#21** — correct is **a** (ねば), not れば. 「返さねばならない」is the fixed V-ない(stem)+ねば+
  ならない pattern; there's no such construction as 〜ればならない — れば is a plain conditional.
- **#23** — correct is **b** (待っては), not 待ったら. 「これ以上待ってはいられない」is the
  て(は)いられない pattern (can't afford to keep doing X); 待ったら is a plain conditional and
  doesn't combine with いられない this way.
- **#24** — correct is **a** (てばかりは), not だけは. 「遊んでばかりはいられない」is the fixed
  particle combination for this pattern; だけは changes the meaning.

**Where this lands:** 13/24 (54%) is a clear step down from a normal day's grammar-choice score.
Nearly every miss above is a same-family mix-up (two patterns that were taught on *different* days
but look/feel similar) rather than a random guess — which is exactly the risk of a cumulative
review day, and exactly why the book schedules one.

**Notes:**
- **#1** — your order gives 最近、遅刻、電車の遅れで、がちだ, which splits がち away from the noun
  it has to directly attach to (遅刻). がち can't be separated from its host word by an inserted
  phrase, unlike a freely-movable adverb — 電車の遅れで has to come *before* 遅刻, not after it.
- **#2** — your order gives 通って、まじめに、いる、ものの, i.e. 通ってまじめにいるものの. まじめに
  has to sit directly in front of the verb it modifies (通う), so it needs to come first:
  まじめに通っているものの.
