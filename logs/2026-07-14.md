## Weak points (Anki candidates)

1. 日付（ひづけ）vs 日常（にちじょう）— same kanji 日, different reading by compound
2. 喫煙所（きつえんじょ）vs 化粧室（けしょうしつ）vs 喫茶店（きっさてん）— cross-item mix-ups within Day 2's kanji set
3. 処分（dispose of one item）vs 分別（sort by category）— had it backwards both ways
4. はる（stick on）vs はがす（peel off）— had it backwards both ways
5. 〜ものなら requires a potential-form verb (戻れる／できる); 〜ものの for concessive with non-potential verbs
6. していただく → the listener/askee performs the action, not the asker (お客様に運んでいただく → 店員が運ぶ)
7. 使役 pattern: [causer]は[affected person]を[verb-させる]
8. 迷惑受身 pattern: [affected person]は[doer]に[verb-られる]て困る/眠れない
