## 文法 — W1D6: 〜ねばならない／〜てはならない／〜て(は)いられない／〜てばかりは(も)いられない — 1/5 (+1 invalid, +1 unanswered), 2/2

### Choice (1/5, +1 invalid, +1 unanswered)
Missed #1 (守らねば, not 守れば — れば doesn't combine with ならない) and #2 (言っては, not 言った
ら — the prohibition pattern needs て+はならない). #4 answered "2" instead of a/b (invalid — same
format slip as Day 5); correct was a (ばかりは). #5 left unanswered; correct was a (てらんない —
casual for て(は)いられない, fits "can't afford the leisure," not てはならない's prohibition sense).
Only #3 (待っては) was answered correctly. All four of today's grammar patterns look similar on
the surface but attach and mean differently — this section needs the most review.

### Reorder (2/2) ✅
#6: my order (仕事を→一日でも早く→覚えねば→ならない) differs from the "textbook" order but is
equally grammatical — 一日でも早く and 仕事を can swap freely before the verb. #7 (もう→遊んで→
ばかりは→いられない) matched exactly.
